Cost Multiplier Equations

1. Grade Penalty (Slope)

Applies to Normal Track and Bridges. Tunnels are exempt (assumed bored).

$$ M_{grade} = e^{(k_{grade} \cdot S_g \cdot Grade\%/100)} $$

2. Curve Penalty (Tight Turns)

$$ Tightness = \max(0, \frac{R_{min}}{R_{actual}} - 1) $$ $$ M_{curve} = e^{(k_{curve} \cdot S_c \cdot Tightness)} $$

3. Elevation Penalty (Tunnels Only)

$$ ExcessE = \max(0, Elev_{max} - E0) $$ $$ M_{elev} = e^{(k_{elev} \cdot \frac{ExcessE}{100})} $$
Penalizes deep tunnels under high mountains (Threshold E0).

4. Length Penalty (Long Bridges/Tunnels)

$$ ExcessL = \max(0, Length_{structure} - L0) $$ $$ M_{len} = 1 + c_{type} \cdot k_{len} \cdot \frac{ExcessL}{1000} \; (capped) $$
Long continuous structures attract a capped length premium rather than an unbounded exponential penalty (Threshold L0).

Quick Start Guide

1. Plan the route
Click on the map to place control points. To protect the public elevation server from overload, each new point is limited to 20 km from the previous selection.

2. Build the route
Switch to Build mode and paint route sections as normal track, bridge, tunnel, or cut/fill.

3. Check the profile
Review the elevation profile and warnings. Red sections indicate grade, curvature, or vertical alignment issues.

4. Compare costs
Use the cost estimator to compare route options and justify your final selection.

Engineering Tools

1. Mode Selection

Click to add, drag to move, right-click to delete.

2. Construction Type

Paint: Drag to paint when range mode is off.
Range Fill: Click start, then click end.

3. Design Parameters

Required Radius: ~4090 m

4. Exports & Data

Session files restore the route, painted structures, design settings, and local KML contours.
